Battleship!

In battleship you and your opponent will each have 5 boats.

You will begin by placing your boats however you want in your grid, however they cannot overlap each other or overhang the grid.

You will then take turns selecting a square on each others board, and it will reveal if you have hit a boat or just water.

You continue until someone entire fleet has been sunk!
